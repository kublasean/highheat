About

Don't Let Me Down was recorded, mixed, and mastered at Standard Electric Recorders Co. by Damon Moon. The song features:

  • Freddy Bahati - Lead guitar
  • Daniel Sawyer - Bass and a lead guitar solo
  • Sean Whalen - Drums
