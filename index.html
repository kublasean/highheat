HIGH HEAT brings a new voice to rock 'n' roll music in Knoxville Tennessee. Are you ready to hear it?

{% include sticker_logo.html %}

Since meeting at the University of Tennessee, we've been crafting our sound for the better part of a decade.

That sound is inspired by the craftsmanship of the classic rock gods of old and blended with the modern, poignant storytelling of indie rock. The wide range of our influences leads us to write songs that intersperse blazing guitar solos and dynamic unison breakdowns with catchy choruses and meaningful lyrics. It’s controlled chaos that feels raw and genuine without sacrificing quality.

High Heat at Lakeside Studios with Grammy nominated, award-winning mixer, engineer, and producer Travis Wyrick.

Travis Wyrick has worked with incredible artists such as The Dirty Guv’nahs, Dolly Parton, P.O.D., and many more. We can’t wait for you to hear the material from this session, to be released later in 2022.
